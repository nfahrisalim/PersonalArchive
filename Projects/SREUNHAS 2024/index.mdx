This is a website for SRE Universitas Hasanuddin, showcasing what we are up to, about us, our achievements, and our latest news. SRE stands for **Society Of Renewable Energy**, an organization dedicated to promoting renewable energy awareness and education at Universitas Hasanuddin.

The website was developed by the **Subdivisi Web Development** in **2024**, featuring a modern and responsive design that provides comprehensive information about the organization's activities, mission, and accomplishments. The site serves as the primary digital presence for SRE UNHAS, connecting students, faculty, and the broader community with renewable energy initiatives and educational content.
